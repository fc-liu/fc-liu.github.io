About me

Hello! My name is Fangchao Liu. I am currently a PhD candidate at ICIP(ISCAS, under the supervision of Professor Xianpei Han and Professor Le Sun. Before this, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Software Institute, Nanjing University.

My research interests focus on information extraction, especially on relation extraction, including methodologies and models for few-shot and open relation extraction.
